# Child Safety Standards - Whispr

**Last updated:** June 2026

## Our Commitment
Whispr is committed to protecting children from sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We have zero tolerance for any content or behavior that exploits or endangers minors.

## Prevention Measures
- Users must be 13+ to use the app
- Automated content filtering for inappropriate material
- All reported content is reviewed within 24 hours
- Repeat offenders are permanently banned

## Reporting
Users can report inappropriate content or behavior directly in the app:
- Tap the three dots (...) on any post
- Select "Report"
- Choose a reason for reporting
