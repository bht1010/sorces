Privacy Policy for Whispr

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Introduction

Whispr ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Email address - for account creation (optional, anonymous mode available)
  • User Content - posts, comments, messages, and profile bio
  • Anonymous Username - randomly generated for your profile

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Device Token - for push notifications
  • App Usage Data - basic interaction data (likes, comments, follows)

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Real name
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Photos or media files
  • Contacts
  • Browsing history

How We Use Your Information

  • App Functionality: To enable posting, commenting, messaging, and social features
  • Notifications: To send push notifications about interactions
  • Account Management: To authenticate and manage your account
  • Improvement: To fix bugs and improve the app experience

Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored on secure servers using MongoDB Atlas
  • Communications are encrypted via HTTPS
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data
  • Data is retained only as long as your account exists

Third-Party Services

  • MongoDB Atlas: Cloud database storage
  • Render: Backend server hosting
  • Expo Push Notifications: Push notification delivery

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Disable push notifications
  • Stop using the app at any time

To delete your account, use the logout option in the app.

Children's Privacy

Whispr is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Users will be notified by updating the "Last updated" date.
