# Privacy Policy for Whispr

**Last updated:** May 28, 2026

---

## Introduction

Whispr ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Email address**: Used for account creation and login (optional - you can use anonymous mode)
- **User Content**: Posts, comments, messages, and profile bio you create
- **Anonymous Username**: Randomly generated username for your profile

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Token**: For push notifications
- **App Usage Data**: Basic interaction data (likes, comments, follows)

---

## Information We DO NOT Collect

- Real name
- Phone number
- Location data
- Photos or media
- Contacts
- Browsing history

---

## How We Use Your Information

- **App Functionality**: To enable posting, commenting, messaging, and social features
- **Notifications**: To send push notifications about interactions (likes, comments, messages)
- **Account Management**: To authenticate and manage your account
- **Improvement**: To fix bugs and improve the app experience

---

## Data Storage & Security

- All data is stored on secure servers using MongoDB Atlas
- Communications are encrypted via HTTPS
- We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data
- We retain your data only as long as your account exists

---

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **MongoDB Atlas**: Cloud database storage
- **Render**: Backend server hosting
- **Expo Push Notifications**: Push notification delivery

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Disable push notifications
- Stop using the app at any time

To delete your account and data, use the logout option in the app.

---

## Children's Privacy

Whispr is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
